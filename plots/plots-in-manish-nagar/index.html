Are these Manish Nagar plots NMRDA sanctioned?

Yes. All our plots in and around Manish Nagar are NMRDA approved with Release Letters (RL). We ensure clear titles and 100% transparent documentation.

How is the connectivity from Manish Nagar to MIHAN?

Manish Nagar is just 5 minutes away from MIHAN and the Nagpur Airport. The new flyovers and Metro connectivity make it the most reachable residential hub.
