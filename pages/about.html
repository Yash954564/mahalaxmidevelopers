Who We Are

Building Trust Across Generations

Mahalaxmi Group is one of Nagpur's most reputed real estate developers, with a legacy spanning over 15 years. We specialize in creating premium residential and commercial plotted developments that combine strategic locations, verified approvals, and world-class amenities.

With 67+ completed projects spread across 880+ acres and a family of 15,000+ happy customers, we've built a reputation for transparency, quality, and trust. Every project we undertake is backed by clear titles, RERA compliance, and NMRDA/NIT sanctions.

Our mission is simple: to make plot ownership accessible, transparent, and rewarding for every family in Nagpur and beyond.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in Nagpur's real estate market by delivering plotted developments that exceed customer expectations in quality, location, and value appreciation.

Our Mission

To transform Nagpur's real estate landscape through innovation, transparency, and customer-first approach. We aim to create communities that families are proud to call home.
