Plot Investment FAQ

Investing in Nagpur Plotted Development

Finding the right plot requires the right information. We've compiled the most common questions our investors ask.

Need a Guided Tour?

Our experts can take you through multiple projects in one evening. Book your personalized site visit now.

Why invest in Nagpur's peripheral growth areas?

Areas like Samruddhi Mahamarg, Wardha Road, and Besa are witnessing rapid infrastructure development. With the Metro expansion and MIHAN growth, these areas offer the highest appreciation potential in Nagpur.

How much loan can I get for a residential plot?

For NMRDA/NIT sanctioned plots, most banks offer up to 75-80% loan. However, through our strategic banking partners, Upto 90% loan finance facilities are available for eligible profiles.

What is RL (Release Letter) and why is it important?

RL is a document issued by the planning authority (NIT/NMRDA) confirming that the layout is sanctioned and the plot is ready for construction. All our projects come with valid RLs.

Do you provide help with the registration process?

Yes, our dedicated documentation team handles everything from agreement drafting to final registration at the Sub-Registrar office, ensuring a hassle-free experience for you.

What are the development charges?

Development charges cover infrastructure like roads, drainage, water, and electricity. We provide a transparent breakup of all charges at the time of booking with no hidden costs.
