FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you book a site visit.

Are these plots RERA & NMRDA approved?

Yes. All our projects are 100% NMRDA sanctioned and RERA registered. we provide all necessary documents including the 7/12 extract and sanctioned layout maps early in the process.

Can I get a bank loan for these plots?

Yes. We have tie-ups with nationalized and private banks like SBI, HDFC, and ICICI. We facilitate Upto 90% loan finance facilities for all our premium residential projects in Nagpur.

What amenities do you provide in your layouts?

Our premium developments feature cement roads, underground drainage, water pipelines, electricity with street lights, gated security, and beautifully landscaped gardens or open spaces.

How far are the projects from Nagpur city?

Our projects are strategically located in high-growth corridors like Wardha Road, Besa, and Samruddhi Mahamarg, typically within 10-20 minutes of the main city center and key hubs like MIHAN and AIIMS.

Is there any hidden cost involved?

No. We maintain absolute transparency. All costs including plot price, registration, and development charges are clearly communicated during the consultation.
