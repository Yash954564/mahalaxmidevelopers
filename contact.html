How to Choose the Right Plot?

Choosing a residential plot in Nagpur is a significant investment. Here's what we recommend checking before you buy:

1

Verify Approvals

Always look for NMRDA/NIT sanctioned projects with RERA registration. This ensures your investment is legally protected.

2

Check Connectivity

Proximity to developing hubs like MIHAN, Samruddhi Mahamarg, and Wardha Road ensures high appreciation in property value.

3

Loan Eligibility

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

Are these plots RERA & NMRDA approved?

Yes. All our current residential and commercial projects are fully NMRDA sanctioned and RERA registered. We provide complete documentation for legal verification.

Do you provide bank loan assistance?

Absolutely. We have tie-ups with major nationalized and private banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, etc.). We facilitate Upto 90% loan finance for eligible customers.

What is the buying process?

The process starts with a site visit, followed by selecting your plot, agreement to sale, and finally the registration. Our legal team assists with every step.

Where are your projects located?

Our projects are strategically located in high-growth areas of Nagpur, including Samruddhi Mahamarg, Wardha Road, Besa, and Shankarpur.

Is there any hidden cost?

No. We maintain absolute transparency. All costs (plot price, registration charges, development charges) are discussed upfront before booking.
